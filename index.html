Running: the pinnacle sport

One of the oldest sports known to man, running is the original cardio exercise. runhappy highlights this sport's unique features of accessibility, fitness, health, social, and mental benefits via the adventures of a lifelong cross country runner.

Unlike other physical activities, a run can be enjoyed virtually anywhere and at anytime one pleases - whether an indoor or outdoor preference, city streets and nature trails everywhere are available for exploration. With the cost-effectiveness of requiring minal gear and ability to set a unique pace, goal, and mindset, people of any skill and fitness level can pick up the activity and experience the epiphany of a swift stride!
